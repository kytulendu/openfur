# Fursuit claw

![Screenshot](./fursuit_claw/screenshot_claw.png)

3D printable fursuit claw for your fursuit handpaw.

I use Cura to slice the model and print with Ender-5 Plus.

Print settings
- Scale: 10000%
- Infill Density: 80%
- Support: No
- Build Plate Adhesion Type: Brim
